Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2015, TentCo started with a simple mission: create high-quality, affordable camping tents that inspire people to explore the great outdoors.

Our team of experienced designers and outdoor enthusiasts work tirelessly to innovate and perfect every tent we produce. We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable gear that won't break the bank.
